UPSIDA In Brief

UPSIDA is the nodal agency for developing industrial infrastructure in the state. Till date UPSIDA has developed 150+ industrial areas

Uttar Pradesh has been a leader in the industrial sector, and we have achieved this position with consistent efforts. We have taken right steps and in effect industries are not just coming to Uttar Pradesh from other states, but huge investments are also flowing in from overseas. Foreign investors are eager to invest in various sectors in the state after being convinced of the change in culture and ease of doing business.
